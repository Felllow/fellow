About Us

Fellow was founded by five Creative Business students who recognized the importance of social connections amongst seniors. The current growing online world we live in makes that the search and preservation of social connections not always come naturally anymore for elderly people. At Fellow, we are offering a helping hand for seniors in the digital environment. The platform and device we created provides an easy and accessible way for seniors to connect with others. Our mission is to bridge the gap between loneliness and community.

Vision

Our vision is to create a world where seniors can maintain their social connections and reconnect with their generation in a simple way. We believe that through these connections, they can preserve their mental and emotional well-being.

‘’In a world where seniors can feel isolated and disconnected from their loved ones and the community around them, We believe that there should be a simple way for them to maintain their social connections and reconnect with their generation, Because only then can they preserve their mental and emotional well-being.’’

Values

Connection

We believe that every senior should have access to meaningful connections. Our brand values the importance of human relationships and strives to provide an environment that fosters and maintains social connections.

Simplicity

We aim to make technology easy and accessible for seniors. Our brand values simplicity in all aspects of our product from the design to the user interface, to ensure that every user can easily connect with others.

Community

We understand that community is crucial for a fulfilling life. Our brand values the importance of creating a sense of belonging and inclusivity for all seniors who use our product.

Guidance

We are committed to providing guidance and support to our users. Our brand values the importance of offering a helping hand to ensure that every user has a positive experience while using our product.
